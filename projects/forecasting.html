Advanced Forecasting Models

Implemented diverse forecasting models, including ARIMA, Bayesian Time Series, and Prophet, to provide stakeholders with robust, data-driven forecasts. This approach enhanced accuracy in predicting outcomes for experiments and key performance indicators, supporting strategic decisions.

Project Overview

This project was centered on enhancing the forecasting capabilities within the organization. By implementing a range of advanced models, we aimed to improve the accuracy and reliability of our predictions for various business metrics and experiment outcomes.
